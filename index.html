Project Goals

Gotta Minute Fit Lab aims to revolutionize fitness for busy individuals by combining 3D body scan technology with AI-driven personalized workout plans. Our goal is to deliver quick, effective 1-minute workout routines tailored to each user's body composition and fitness objectives, complete with progress tracking and nutritional guidance.

Key Features

  • 3D body scan technology for precise body composition analysis
  • Quick 1-minute workout routines designed for busy schedules
  • AI-powered customizable fitness plans
  • Target area focused exercises
  • Progress tracking and analytics
  • Personalized meal plan suggestions

Technologies Used

  • 3D body scan modeling
  • AI and machine learning algorithms
  • Web and mobile application development
  • Data analytics for progress tracking
