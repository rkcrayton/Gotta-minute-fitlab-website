Smarter fitness, one minute at a time

Gotta Minute Fit Lab aims to revolutionize fitness for busy individuals by combining 3D body scan technology with AI-driven personalized workout plans. Our goal is to deliver quick, effective one-minute workouts tailored to each user’s body composition and fitness objectives—supported by progress tracking and nutritional guidance.

Senior Design • Spring 2026

Mobile Body Scan

Perform a quick body scan directly from a smartphone to capture posture, movement, and physical measurements.

3D Body Reconstruction

Dynamically reconstruct a 3D body model from scan data to analyze form, symmetry, and movement patterns.

Target-Area Identification

Identify areas requiring increased strength, improved mobility, or fat reduction using scan-based analysis.

Personalized Exercise Plans

Generate workouts using height, weight, occupation, and body-scan data, tailored for home or gym environments.

One-Minute Workouts

Deliver time-efficient workouts designed to fit seamlessly into a busy schedule.

Daily Prompts & Progress Tracking

Notify users throughout the day to complete short workouts and track total activity to encourage consistency.

Meal Suggestions

Provide suggested meals that align with the user’s fitness goals and activity level.
